*** This is the front-end part of a online watch store (Personal project)

*** It is just a snippet and the project may have incomplete parts.

*** Built with Bootstrap 3, HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery.


*** UPDATE 19 March 2018 *** - Added the 'add to cart' functionality'
- You can add or remove from cart any product on the site
- When you click on the cart, a modal dialog appears which shows you the products
- When you add/remove a product from the cart a notification shows on the upper right corner
